Frequently Asked Questions

What is an open box product?

Open box products are items that have been returned by customers or used as display models. They are thoroughly inspected, tested, and repackaged to ensure they meet our high standards of quality.

Yes, we offer a limited warranty on all our products. The duration and terms of the warranty vary depending on the product. Please contact us for specific warranty information.
