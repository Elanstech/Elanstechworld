Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms or custom applications may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your pricing?

All projects include custom design, development, mobile optimization, SEO setup, training, and 3-6 months of support. No hidden fees—everything is transparent from day one.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Absolutely! We offer monthly support packages that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and priority technical support. Your success is our success.

Can you integrate with my existing systems?

Yes! We specialize in integrations—Square, Stripe, Mailchimp, CRMs, scheduling systems, and more. We'll ensure everything works seamlessly together.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

We offer unlimited revisions during the development phase and a 100% satisfaction guarantee. We don't stop working until you're absolutely thrilled with the result.
