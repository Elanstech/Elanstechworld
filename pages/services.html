Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The development timeline varies based on complexity. A basic app typically takes 2-3 months, while complex apps can take 4-6 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Should I develop for iOS, Android, or both?

This depends on your target audience. If your users are primarily on one platform, you might start there. However, most businesses benefit from being on both platforms. We can help you make this decision based on your specific goals and budget.

What's the difference between native and cross-platform development?

Native apps are built specifically for one platform (iOS or Android) using platform-specific languages. Cross-platform apps use frameworks like React Native or Flutter to deploy to multiple platforms from a single codebase. Native apps typically offer better performance and access to platform features, while cross-platform development can be more cost-effective and faster to market.

Do you handle app store submissions?

Yes, we provide comprehensive assistance with app store submissions, including preparing all necessary assets, documentation, and addressing any feedback from app store reviewers.
