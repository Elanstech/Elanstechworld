Got Questions?

Services FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including web design and development, mobile app development, POS system implementation, digital marketing, Google Ads management, property management systems, print and graphic design, and technology consulting. Our goal is to provide end-to-end solutions that meet all your technology needs.

How much does a website or app typically cost?

The cost of a website or app varies depending on your specific requirements, the complexity of the project, and the features needed. We offer customized solutions tailored to your business needs and budget. Contact us for a free consultation and personalized quote based on your project specifications.

How long does it take to build a website?

A basic website typically takes 3-4 weeks to complete, while more complex websites with custom functionality can take 8-12 weeks. The timeline depends on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements of your project. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your needs.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Responsive design is a standard feature in all our web development packages.

Do you develop for both iOS and Android?

Yes, we develop mobile applications for both iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android platforms. We can create native apps specifically for each platform or cross-platform apps using frameworks like React Native or Flutter that work on both platforms from a single codebase, depending on your project requirements and budget.

Which POS systems do you implement?

We implement various POS systems including Square, Clover, Shopify POS, Lightspeed, Toast, and Vend. We'll recommend the best solution based on your business type, size, and specific needs. We handle everything from hardware selection and software setup to staff training and ongoing support.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
