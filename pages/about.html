Who We Are

Our Journey

The story of passion, innovation, and dedication

2018

The Beginning

Elan's Tech World was founded with a simple mission: to make technology accessible, understandable, and valuable for businesses of all sizes. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a diverse team of experts.

2020

Expanding Horizons

As the world adapted to new challenges, we expanded our services to help businesses navigate the digital transformation. We doubled our team and launched our comprehensive POS and property management solutions.

2022

Innovation Breakthrough

We reached a significant milestone with the development of our integrated technology ecosystem approach, allowing businesses to seamlessly connect all their digital touchpoints for maximum efficiency.

Today

Looking to the Future

Today, we continue to innovate and adapt to the ever-changing technological landscape. Our focus remains on helping businesses leverage technology to achieve their goals and create exceptional experiences for their customers.
